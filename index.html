Frequently Asked Questions

What is 15% of 200?

15% of 200 is 30. To calculate: multiply 200 by 0.15 (which is 15 ÷ 100). 200 × 0.15 = 30.

How do I calculate a percentage of a number?

Divide the percentage by 100 to get a decimal, then multiply by the number. For example, 20% of 150 = (20 ÷ 100) × 150 = 0.20 × 150 = 30.

How do I calculate percentage change?

Subtract the original value from the new value, divide by the original value, then multiply by 100. Formula: ((New − Old) ÷ Old) × 100. A positive result is an increase, negative is a decrease.

What is 20% of 50?

20% of 50 is 10. Calculation: 50 × 0.20 = 10.

How do I find what percentage one number is of another?

Divide the first number by the second, then multiply by 100. For example, 30 is what percent of 200? (30 ÷ 200) × 100 = 15%.
