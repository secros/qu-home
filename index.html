About QuickUtils

QuickUtils is a growing collection of free, no-signup utility tools. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — nothing you type is ever sent to a server or stored anywhere.

Whether you need to convert a number to Roman numerals, count the words in an essay, figure out how many days old you are, or translate between binary and hexadecimal — there's a tool here for you.

New tools are added regularly. All free, forever.
